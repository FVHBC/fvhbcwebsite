Our Doctrine

Baptist Faith & Message 2000

Fox Valley Hmong Baptist Church affirms the Baptist Faith and Message 2000 as our doctrinal statement. This document summarizes the core beliefs shared by Southern Baptist churches and serves as a guide for our faith and practice.

The Baptist Faith and Message is rooted in Scripture and addresses essential Christian doctrines including the nature of God, salvation through Jesus Christ, the authority of Scripture, the church, and the Christian's responsibility to share the Gospel with the world.

Read the Full Statement

The Scriptures

The Bible is God's revelation of Himself to man, the perfect treasure of divine instruction.

God the Son

Jesus Christ is the eternal Son of God, who lived a sinless life and died for our salvation.

Salvation

Salvation involves the redemption of the whole man and is offered freely to all who accept Jesus Christ as Lord and Saviour.

The Church

A New Testament church is an autonomous local congregation of baptized believers, united for worship and fellowship.
