About Our Church

Who We Are

A Place Where Faith
Grows Together

At Fox Valley Hmong Baptist Church, we are a Christ-centered community rooted in faith, culture, and love. Whether you've walked with God for decades or are just beginning your journey, you belong here.

We gather every Sunday in Appleton, WI to worship, study the Word, and encourage one another as we strive to fulfill the Great Commission together.

Our Mission

"To fully participate in the fulfillment of the Great Commission"

We are called to go, teach, and baptize — sharing the love of Jesus Christ with our community and the world.

Matthew 28:16-20
Our Vision

"Glorify and Multiply"

Devoted to teaching, fellowship, breaking of bread, and prayer — growing as a community that glorifies God and multiplies disciples.

Acts 2:42-47
