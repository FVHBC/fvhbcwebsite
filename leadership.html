Our Leadership

At Fox Valley Hmong Baptist Church, everything starts with a clear vision and heartfelt passion. Just as a seed grows into a flourishing tree, our church leaders are inspired by faith-given ideas that blossom into impactful ministries, bringing hope and transformation to our community.

Our leaders believe every idea has the power to transform lives — just as your dreams and creativity can light the path forward. Together, we build a church family rooted in faith, love, and purpose.
